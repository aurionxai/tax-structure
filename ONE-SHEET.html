The Seven Moves

Foundation 🏛️

1 · Make two companies

Company One trades. Zero employees. Money (K-1) flows straight to partners — no W-2 required, no payroll tax. Company Two (each partner gets their own) gets paid a fee to "run things" — that fee unlocks retirement caves.

Not optional 📜

2 · File the magic paper ("475")

Fast single-stock options + the dumb "wash sale" rule = owing tax on money never made. Magic paper kills the rule. Losses always count. Single-stock pays full ~40% rate regardless — paper protects, doesn't reduce rate.

⏰ 75 days after company born. Miss = wait whole year.

W-2 rule 💼

3 · Pay yourself a salary (from Company Two only)

Company Two must pay a real W-2 salary (~$150–400K) before distributions. All-distribution = IRS audit bait. The rest flows as distributions — no payroll tax. The big K-1 from Company One? No salary needed at all.

Steady 🦴

4 · Stuff retirement caves

401(k) + big pension plan + mega backdoor Roth (~$70K/yr max into Roth) per partner. Tax pushed to tomorrow or never. Small next to $50M — but free and permanent.

Kids 👶

5 · Kids' Roth IRAs

Employ kids for real work through Company Two. Pay up to ~$15K (zero tax owed). Parent writes the $7K Roth check — IRS doesn't care who writes it, only that kid earned it. 50–60 years of tax-free compounding.

Main weapon 🛡️

6 · The big shield (PPLI)

Special life insurance wrapper. Money inside grows tax-free. Borrow out tax-free. Kids inherit tax-free. One law: you can't drive the trades inside it — independent manager must. Break that, lose everything.

Legacy 🗿

7 · Pass it down smart

Family trusts own the shield → wealth skips the 40% death tax. Charity trusts = big deductions now. Every $100M moved ≈ $40M saved later.

Truth ⚖️

The honest part

Single-stock options pay the full ~40% rate. No legal trick changes that character. Only index options (SPX-kind) get ~27% — move bets there only if it doesn't hurt the edge. Never trade alpha for tax.

🔥

Touch These? Fire. Bad.

Tribes that touched these lost everything:

  • Offshore funds — does nothing for US citizens. IRS sees all. Always.
  • Captive insurance — on the IRS hit list. People lose in court constantly.
  • C-corp "21% trick" — hidden extra taxes make it worse, not better.
  • "Turn fast money into slow money" tricks — famous fund tried. Paid $7,000,000,000 to settle. Biggest tax settlement ever.

Don't do this alone. Hire trader-tax CPA + tax lawyer. Deadlines bite hard and paperwork must be real. Big firms have no secret magic — they do this exact boring stuff, perfectly, every year. That ~18–20% floor? It's where legal ends. Everything below it has a court case attached.
